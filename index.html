2019

Automation of VTVL Rocket

Programmed a miniature model rocket to perform vertical takeoffs and landings. The rocket is powered by potassium nitrate (KNO3) and powdered sugar. The system includes a Raspberry Pi as the control system, with an MPL3115A2 for measuring height, an MPU6050 for stabilization, and an embedded wizard for monitoring rocket performance.
